Target Audience

The target audience for this site are business owners. Whether local or not we want bussinesses to come to our site, and our state.

Persona 1

The first kind of people we want to come to our site are local business owners. We want Colorado businesses to come and join our organization and help promote their business interests and those of other local businesses.

Persona 2

The second kind of people we want to come to our site are those who are planning to move or set up their business in a new location. We want those visitors to see our site and be persuaded to move to Colorado.

Scenarios

  • A local business owner want to know more about the issues that could affect thier bussines
  • A non local business owner who wants to learn about local policies.
  • A student who wants to learn more about chambers of commerce.
  • Locals who are interested in starting a business.
  • People who wish to make their own chamber of commerce page.
